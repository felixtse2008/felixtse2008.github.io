Achievement

I am a volleyball referee since 1st September 2015. I joined a referee course in the last summer and I got a pass in it. After I became a referee, I have participated in over 80 matches in the position of First Referee, Second Referee and Scorer. "Playing" volleyball in different role is very exciting !

My Volleyball Life

My first touch in volleyball was in primary 3. I joined the volleyball team and started training. After a year, I had a chance to take part in the "Primary Schools Sports Competition". A year later, I had been the team captain until I graduated. Unfortunately, there is not a volleyball coach in my secondary school, we can only train ourselves. I also joined competitions from Form 1 to Form 3, sadly, we did not get a good result at all.


After the graduation of secondary study, I do not want to give up my favourite. I chose another way to keep in touch with volleyball, so, I attended the referee course, and I got pass in it finally. I remember my first job was in Mei Lam Sports Centre, but a typhoon signal No. 3 has issued after two competitions, we need to cancel all the remaining competitions. I was only a scorer in that two competitions. I think it is a good to be a referee and keeping in touch with my favourite sport !
